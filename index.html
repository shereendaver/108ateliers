THE FOUNDER

Welcome,

I created 108 Ateliers and its Method from a conviction that this moment of upheaval is the perfect time to shape new possibilities — a hopeful, happier, healthier and fairer way to think, feel, and live.
Twenty years inside global advertising agencies, partnering with Fortune 100 brands worldwide, and building interconnected business systems, has taught me the power of communication and relationships — and that people with agency can effect change at scale. That world demanded listening, understanding and critical thinking; combining that people intelligence with an eye for systems is where my work must begin.
I communicate across worlds, bridging science and spirituality, theology and energy. My pursuit of ancient knowledge and modern longevity is part of an ever-expanding arsenal of perspectives in service of human-positive ways of living and working.
My gift is navigating blockages: identifying the true problem so that systemic solutions can emerge. 108 Method carries the same belief — that we are interconnected, part of both problem and solution, and that no thought, word or action is too small to matter.
With gratitude, Shereen

Who we are
108 Ateliers ("we", "us") is a studio for considered living. We are the data controller for personal information collected through this website. This policy explains what we collect, why, and the rights you hold under the UK GDPR and the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).
What we collect
When you enquire, apply, or join the waitlist we collect the details you choose to share: your name, email address, and anything you write in your note. We do not collect special-category data, and we ask that you do not include sensitive information in your messages.
Why we use it, and our lawful basis
We use your information to respond to your enquiry, manage applications to the 108 Method and membership, and — only where you have agreed — write to you about future openings and gatherings. Our lawful bases under Article 6 GDPR are: taking steps at your request before entering into a contract (responding to enquiries and applications), consent (any ongoing correspondence you opt into), and legitimate interest (running and improving the studio). We never sell your data.
Who we share it with
Your information stays within 108 Ateliers. We use a small number of trusted service providers — such as email and website hosting — who process data on our behalf under contract, and only as needed to run this site. Where a provider is outside the UK or EEA, transfers are protected by appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses.
How long we keep it
We keep enquiries and application details only as long as needed to hold the conversation and administer cohorts, then delete them. If you ask us to remove your information at any point, we will.
Your rights
Under the GDPR you have the right to access the personal data we hold about you; to correct it; to have it erased; to restrict or object to our processing; to data portability; and to withdraw consent at any time. To exercise any of these, simply write to us. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your supervisory authority — in the UK, the Information Commissioner's Office (ico.org.uk).
Cookies
This site does not use advertising or tracking cookies. Any local storage is used only to make the site work in your browser.
Contact
Questions about this policy or your data: write to us here. We may update this policy from time to time; the date above reflects the latest version.
